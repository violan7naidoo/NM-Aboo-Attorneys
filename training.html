Bespoke Courses

We offer bespoke training courses on Quality Management Systems, Environmental Management Systems, Health and Safety Management Systems, Supply Chain Management and Business Risk Management to the public and private sectors of the SA economy.

Lean Six Sigma

We offer Lean Six Sigma (Yellow, Green and Black Belt) progressive training, preparing students to write online exams to achieve these qualifications through institutes of their preference.

3.

Certification Bodies

We execute business systems training on behalf of Certification Bodies, as may be requested by them.

Local and Global Training

We work closely with the Institute of Packaging SA (IPSA) and the World Packaging Organisation (WPO) to conduct local and global training on their behalf.

5.

Technical Workshops For Businesses

We work with universities in SA to facilitate technical workshops for businesses.

6.

Public Workshops

We also conduct periodic public workshops on current business topics, given our business relationships with IPSA, WPO and the SA government
