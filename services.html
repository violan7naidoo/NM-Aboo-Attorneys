1.

What We Offer

We are a broad based consulting firm that is able to offer clients in depth, systems auditing, specifically with respect to the implementation of management systems such as ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45000:2015, FSSC 22000, ISO 31000, ISO 50000, as well as other related management systems.

2.

Practical Business Solutions

We report on such audits and offer practical business solutions for a bespoke implementation of the above systems, together with the training and education support required for third-party certification.

3.

Unique Selling points

Our unique selling point is that the design and integration of ISO systems is driven fundamentally by a triple bottom line approach. Hence business process re-engineering forms an integral part of the scope of our work, where success is measured by economic, social and environmental equity.

4.

Certified

We engage with Certification Bodies (preferred partners are SABS, DNV.GL, DQS, SGS and BVQI) on behalf of the client, as is necessary for third-party certification

5.

Training & Education

Training and Education, in support of ISO system revisions, are value added offerings as and when such ISO systems change. Learn more
