GI Business Solutions

History

Founded in 2013, GI Business Solutions (Pty) Ltd has grown into a significant consulting firm globally, and specialises in the implementation of ISO business management systems designed to meet specific client needs. The unique selling proposition of GI Business Solutions is that processes are designed in support of a triple bottom line approach. Hence, business process re-engineering and process integration is core and results in a balanced economic, social and environmental improvement.
GI Business Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a DNV.GL ISO9001:2015 certified consulting firm.

GI Business Solutions

Scope

GI Business Solutions (Pty) Ltd provides the technical input in the design, development and implementation of management systems to both the service and manufacturing sectors of business. In addition, the firm provides the necessary support training and competence evaluation required for third-party certification. Business systems may be aligned to generic ISO systems or tailor made to suit specific client requirements. The firm also conducts technical training and competence evaluation on behalf of local and global clients.
